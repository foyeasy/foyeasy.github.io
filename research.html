Undergraduate Thesis

Mechanical Engineering, Shahjalal University of Science and Technology

Title: Enhancing Cutting Tool Durability by Addressing Thermal Effects: A Numerical Study.
Supervisor: MD. MAHMUD-OR-RASHID, Assistant Professor, Mechanical Engineering, SUST

  • ◦ Investigated conjugate heat transfer in cutting inserts using COMSOL Multiphysics to minimize thermal effects and enhance tool life.
  • ◦ Introduced two key cutting parameters: the angle of attack of the coolant and the insert hole size.
  • ◦ Modeled a non-conventional cutting insert with a conductive core to reduce cutting temperature.
  • ◦ This research resulted in publications in both a journal and a conference proceedings, with a third in preparation.
